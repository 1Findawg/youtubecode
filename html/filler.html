1Findawg - Filler

Introduction:

Filler is a two player game. Player 1 starts in the top right corner and Player 2 starts in the bottom left corner. Each player takes turns picking a color. When they pick a color all of their squares will change to that color. Any adjacent squares of the same coloer will be added to their squares and score. Diagonal squars do not get added, only squares that share a side with squares the player already has.
