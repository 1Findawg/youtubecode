1Findawg - Mastermind

Introduction:

This is is a game where a code (sequence of colors) is automatically randomly selected and hidden. Then the codebreaker gets to have 10 chances to guess the code. After each guess the codebreaker get clues about how well they did. For every red circle next to the submit button they have have 1 slot of the code correct. For every white circle next to the submit button they have 1 correct color but in the wrong position. If the codebreaker guesses correctly in 10 turns or less they win. Note: Start at the bottom most row.
